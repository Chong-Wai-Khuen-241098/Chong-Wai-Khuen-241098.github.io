About Diorlying

Diorlying, whose real name is Tan Jia Ying, was born on February 26, 1998, in Johor, Batu Pahat, Malaysia. She is a talented singer-songwriter who started her musical journey at a young age. Growing up in a creative environment, she discovered her passion for music early on and began writing and performing her own songs. Her unique voice and emotional depth quickly earned her a place in the Malaysian music scene.

Diorlying rose to fame with her heartfelt songs that resonated with many. In 2024, she released her debut single, If That Call Came Through (《如果那通电话有接通》), a song dedicated to her late mother. This emotional track struck a chord with listeners and helped establish her as a promising artist. Later in the year, she released See You Someday (《有天会再相见》), a song written in memory of the late son of influencers Jasmine and Kvin, as well as to remember her own mother. The song's emotional weight and beautiful melody made it a fan favorite.

In May 2024, Diorlying published her song If That Call Came Through (《如果那通电话有接通》), which quickly gained popularity. She won the "Contemporary Pop Playlist" award at the TikTok Music Awards in December 2024 for this track, marking a major milestone in her career.

Later in 2024, Diorlying released another song called Different Blooming Seasons (《花期不同》), which carries a message about growth and accepting that everyone progresses at their own pace.

In February 2025, Diorlying released Ah Dai (《阿呆》), a light-hearted song dedicated to her favorite pillow, “Ah Dai.” This playful and fun track showed her versatility as an artist and allowed her to connect with her fans in a different way.

Aside from her music career, Diorlying is also the founder of Lost Control Sdn Bhd, a music company she started in 2024. This move shows her ambition to shape and influence the music industry beyond just her own musical creations.

On a personal note, Diorlying experienced a significant breakup with her former partner, Firdaus. Despite the heartache, she poured her emotions into her music, using it as a form of healing and growth. Her songs continue to reflect the deep feelings she has experienced in her personal life, resonating with fans who relate to her stories of love, loss, and self-discovery.

Through her music, Diorlying continues to inspire and uplift those around her. She is an artist who is not afraid to share her emotions and life experiences with the world. Fans admire her authenticity and her ability to turn personal pain into powerful, relatable music.

As Diorlying’s career continues to rise, her music will undoubtedly leave a lasting impact, and fans eagerly await what she will release next.
