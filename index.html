Welcome to Diorlying's Fan Page

Diorlying is a singer from Malaysia. Since she started, she has touched many hearts with her music and unique voice.

Music Releases & Achievements

"If That Call Came Through" (《如果那通电话有接通》)

Release Date: May 2024

About: A song for her late mother, full of deep emotions.

Award: In December 2024, it won the "Contemporary Pop Playlist" award at the TikTok Music Awards.

"See You Someday" (《有天会再相见》)

Release Date: September 2024

About: This song was written to remember a mother's love for her late son, Enzo, and also as a tribute to Diorlying's own mother. It expresses deep emotions of love, loss, and longing.

"Different Blooming Seasons" (《花期不同》)

Release Date: November 2024

About: A song that tells people everyone grows at their own pace, and that's okay.

"Ah Dai" (《阿呆》)

Release Date: February 26, 2025

About: A fun song about her favorite pillow, "Ah Dai."
